Calligraphy Animation Comparison

Comparing three layout-rendering options side-by-side. The flourishes have been restored and the viewBox expanded to prevent clipping.

1. Outline Trace Reveal

Traces the outline of all letterforms and decorative flourishes, then fades into a solid calligraphy stroke.

2. Gradient Sweep Reveal

Masks the artwork with a sliding feathered gradient that reveals the letters and flourishes from left to right.

3. Sequential Stroke Reveal

Measures path coordinates and draws each calligraphy stroke sequentially from left to right.
